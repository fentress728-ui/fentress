About J

Freemasonry is the oldest and largest world wide fraternity of men dedicated to the Brotherhood of Man under the Fatherhood of a Supreme Being.

Based on a system concerned with moral and spiritual values, it teaches each member, by way of symbols and allegory, the duties he owes to his God, his family, his neighbor, and himself.

It instills in its members the virtues of Charity, Brotherly Love, Patriotism, and Friendship in the Lodge as well as in the world at large.

Members of the Fraternity are also taught to be active in their communities and practice patience, kindness, honesty in business, courtesy in society, and fairness in all things.
