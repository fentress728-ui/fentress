Contact Information

You may email us with all of your questions or inquiries.

Jamestown Lodge No. 281 is located in beautiful Jamestown, Tennessee and we invite you to come by and visit any time.

Our monthly stated meetings are on the second Saturday of every month at 7pm Central Standard Time.

Lodge practices are held at 6pm CST each Tuesday. We invite all brethren to attend our practices. We assure you that you will have fun and learn during the practices.

The Jamestown Order of the Eastern Star meets the third Thursday of each month.
