TDMMC

Chapter 11 Three Degrees Masonic Motorcycle Club

Chapter 11 Monthly Stated Meetings are held on the Second Saturday of each month at 5:00pm at Jamestown Lodge No. 281 in Jamestown, TN.

After checking out the News and Events Page from the link above, remember to click “Back” on your browser to return to this page or look for the Chapter 11 web link on the page to return here.

Chapter 11 of the Three Degrees Masonic Motorcycle Club (TDMMC) received their Charter on November 17, 2012 at Jamestown Lodge No. 281 by State Grand Chapter President Loyd Parnell and State Grand Chapter Secretary Joe Carver. Several other members of other State Chapters were in attendance and are to be Thanked for making the long journey and showing their support for the new Chapter.
